Privacy Policy for Darkwave Sleep

Last updated: December 12, 2025

Darkwave Sleep (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Darkwave Sleep mobile application (the “App”).

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The App works entirely offline and does not require user accounts, registration, or network access.

The App does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Data Storage

All app settings (such as volume levels and timer preferences) are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
